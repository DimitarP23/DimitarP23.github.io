    • WHY DID I CHOOSE TO STUDY ICT?

    I chose ICT because it allows me to channel my creativity into crafting innovative solutions and designs using digital tools and technology, fulfilling my passion for imaginative expression in the digital realm.Additionally,I am excited about the prospect of connecting with like-minded individuals who share similar ambitions for their future and personal growth.

    WHAT INITIATIVES DID I TAKE TO MAKE MY STUDY CHOICE?

    Before choosing to study ICT in HZ i talked with students who were already going through their first/second year.They gave me a lot of information on how the program is structured,way of teaching and prospects of studying it. Ofcourse i also did a diligent online research, and comprehensive exploration of career opportunities within the field in order for me to get a better understanding of the ICT field itself.

    MY MOTIVATION

    I am motivated not only by the fact that i see myself as a creative person and creativity is the main unit of ICT, but also the fact that last year i studied a program that was definitely not for me and failed to complete it.I did lost a whole year but i am happy that i know i tried and it wasnt my thing.I am also motivated by the thought that without education it is very difficult to live nowadays and ICT offers great career opportunities and will continue to do so.My motivation also comes from my friends and family and ofcourse from myself-i know that this time i have choosen the right program and feel motivated enough and 100% certain that i will graduate from it.
