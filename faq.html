FAQ'S

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

Please navigate to http://print.hz.nl, and log in using your username and password. Click on "Choose File" to select the file you wish to print. You can repeat this step if you need to add more documents. Once you have selected all the documents, check the "Advanced" option to specify whether you want double-sided or black & white printing. Next, choose either the "HZ printer" or "HZ plotter." After initiating the print request, you'll need to register using your HZ pass at the TouchID station located next to the multifunctional printer, and then select "Print." In the menu, choose the printer for which you submitted the print job. Please note that the print will proceed only if you have sufficient credit in your printing account. Once the printing is finished, press "Stop" on the TouchID, and finally, click "Logout."

Access https://hzuniversity.topdesk.net/tas/public/ssp/ and click on "Make a reservation," followed by "Reserve a room." Select the specific room you wish to book and view the available time slots for reservation.
